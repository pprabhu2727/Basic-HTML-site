Koala Facts! by Prasad Prabhu

Here are some fun facts about Koalas! Koalas can sleep up to 18 hours a day, this is because they don't get much energy from their diet. Koalas survive on a diet of eucalyptus leaves and can eat up to a kilogram a day. Koalas can be found in southeastern and eastern Australia which means they live on one of the driest continents in the world. These facts come from the WWF (World Wildlife Fund) organization and you can learn more about Koalas on their homepage.
